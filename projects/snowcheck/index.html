From idea to prototype

What problem & for whom

For whom: Skiers and snowboarders who check several apps and websites before deciding where to go — they want one place for score, snow type, and a quick compare.

Problem: "Is it worth driving to Baqueira today? What about Cerler?" They need a simple 0–100 score, no sign-up, no clutter.

This: Pick station + today/tomorrow → get score, verdict, 7-day forecast, compare with 2 random stations, webcam links. One click.

Design decisions

Single API (Open-Meteo), no key. All scoring is client-side. Last station in localStorage so returning users get one-tap. Share copies to clipboard.
