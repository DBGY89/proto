# Salad Maker

Pick a few ingredients you have on hand — get a salad recipe that actually tastes good.

## Problem

People want to eat healthier but don't know how to combine random fridge ingredients into something they'd enjoy. Recipe sites require you to already know what you want to make.

## Hypothesis

A simple "pick what you have" interface that generates a balanced salad recipe (base, protein, crunch, dressing) will remove the decision friction that stops people from eating salads.

## Users

Health-conscious people who buy vegetables but let them die in the fridge.

## Planned flow

1. Select ingredients from categorized groups (greens, proteins, toppings, dressings).
2. Hit **Make my salad**.
3. Get a recipe card with proportions and a simple dressing suggestion.

## Metrics to watch

- **Recipe completion**: % of visitors who select ingredients and generate a recipe
- **Ingredient diversity**: avg number of ingredients selected per recipe
- **Return rate**: users who come back to try a different combination
