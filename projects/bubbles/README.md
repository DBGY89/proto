# BUBBLES

A short web game: pop soap bubbles before they float off the screen. Special bubbles burst into glitter. No install, no extension — play in the browser.

## How to play

Click **Play**, then click the bubbles to pop them. You have 45 seconds. Some bubbles are special (they look different) and release a glitter effect when popped. Around 15 seconds in, more bubbles spawn for a short frenzy.

## Stack

HTML, CSS, vanilla JS. Web Audio for pop sounds.
