# Patronus Generator

Answer two questions and discover your Patronus: a spirit animal that represents you (or at least makes you laugh).

## What it does

1. **Question 1** — Where do you recharge? (couch, nature, party, kitchen)
2. **Question 2** — Your secret weapon against the gloom? (humor, silence, hug, the stare)
3. **Result** — A Patronus (emoji + name + funny description). Share via X, WhatsApp, or copy link.

Each combination returns a different animal: cat, owl, fox, otter, dolphin, phoenix, unicorn, dragon, raccoon, hedgehog, bear, lion, and more.

## Style

Same look as the rest of the lab: dark background, neon purple/cyan, Space Grotesk, selectable chips, galaxy orb before reveal, and a share sidebar.

## Status

Standalone project. No backend; all HTML, CSS, and JS run in the client.
